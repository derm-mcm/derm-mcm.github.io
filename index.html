    About Me

    I am currently undertaking a PhD under the supervision of Andrew Jackson (@andrewljackson) as part of the Complex Ecological and Evolutionary Systems research group as a member of the Trinity Centre for Biodiversity Research.

    Research Interests

    My interests lie in understanding the role pf physics in constraining predator-prey interactions at the level of individual animals, in order to gain a fuller understanding of observed macroecological and evolutionary patterns. I approach these questions using a combination of computational and statistical techniques, primarily using MatLab, Python, and the R statistical language.

    I'm an active proponent of science communication. I regularly write for the award-winning EcoEvo@TCD blog and am currently its administrator.
